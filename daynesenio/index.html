About Us

Darren Dayne J. Salay

Senior Architect

Daynesenio Architects and Design was founded in 2025 in Rodriguez, Rizal, growing from a small studio to a trusted team. We specialize in residential, commercial, and community projects, blending modern design with local context and sustainability. Guided by creativity, collaboration, sustainability, integrity, we build spaces that enhance quality of life and reflect clients' visions.

Goals

  • Expand Sustainable Design Leadership

    • Become a recognized leader in eco-friendly architecture in Calabarzon by integrating renewable energy solutions and locally sourced materials into 80% of our projects within the next 3 years.

  • Strengthen Community Partnerships

    • Launch 5 community-focused initiatives (such as affordable housing prototypes or public space upgrades) to create meaningful, accessible structures that serve local neighborhoods.

  • Elevate Client Experience & Delivery

    • Achieve 95% client satisfaction rates and reduce project timeline delays by 20% through enhanced collaboration tools and streamlined design-to-construction processes.
